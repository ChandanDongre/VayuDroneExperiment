VayuDrone India - Documentation

Introduction

VayuDrone India is a web application that allows farmers to book drone services for their farms. The application is designed to be simple and easy to use, even for users who are not familiar with technology.

Getting Started

To get started with VayuDrone India, you will need to create an account. You can do this by clicking the "Get Started" button on the welcome screen and then following the instructions on the register screen.

Booking a Drone Service

Once you have created an account, you can book a drone service by clicking the "Book Drone Service" button on the home screen. You will then be asked to provide the details of the service you want to book, including the type of service, the farm location, the address, the preferred date, and the time of day.
